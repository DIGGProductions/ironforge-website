What is a certified vehicle scale for, and why would you drive to one on purpose? The short answer is to know your weight before it costs you. Here is when it matters.

## Avoiding overweight

An overweight truck is a fine and a downtime risk at the same time. A certified weight before you roll tells you exactly where you stand, while you can still do something about it.

## Axle weights, not just gross

You can be legal on gross weight and still be over on an axle. A scale shows you the split, so you can shift the load instead of getting turned around at a checkpoint.

## Loads priced by weight

If what you haul is priced or limited by weight, a certified number protects you on both ends of the deal.
